Brewing Guide

Perfect your cup with our expert tips

Temperature

Brew at 195-205°F (90-96°C) for optimal extraction and flavor balance

Brew Time

4-5 minutes for French press, 20-30 seconds for espresso shots

Coffee Ratio

Use 1:15 ratio - 1 gram of coffee per 15ml of water for best results
